What We Offer

At FlexWell, we provide a comprehensive range of physiotherapy services designed to help you recover, rehabilitate, and optimize your physical performance. Our evidence-based treatments are tailored to your individual needs.

💪

General Physiotherapy

Comprehensive assessment and treatment for musculoskeletal conditions, including back pain, neck pain, joint problems, and mobility issues.

  • Manual therapy techniques
  • Exercise prescription
  • Pain management
  • Postural correction
🏃‍♂️

Sports Therapy

Specialized treatment for athletes and active individuals, focusing on injury prevention, performance enhancement, and rapid return to sport.

  • Sports injury assessment
  • Performance optimization
  • Injury prevention programs
  • Return-to-sport protocols
🔄

Rehabilitation

Comprehensive rehabilitation programs for post-surgical recovery, neurological conditions, and complex injury management.

  • Post-operative care
  • Neurological rehabilitation
  • Functional restoration
  • Adaptive equipment training
🙌

Manual Therapy

Hands-on treatment techniques to restore joint mobility, reduce pain, and improve tissue flexibility through specialized manipulation.

  • Joint mobilization
  • Soft tissue massage
  • Trigger point therapy
  • Myofascial release
🧘‍♀️

Movement Analysis

Advanced biomechanical assessment to identify movement patterns and develop targeted treatment plans for optimal function.

  • Gait analysis
  • Postural assessment
  • Movement screening
  • Ergonomic evaluation
⚡

Pain Management

Comprehensive approach to chronic pain management using multiple therapeutic modalities and evidence-based techniques.

  • Chronic pain programs
  • Pain education
  • Therapeutic modalities
  • Lifestyle modification

Treatment Modalities

📋 Exercise Therapy

Customized exercise programs designed to restore strength, flexibility, and function specific to your condition and goals.

🔥 Electrotherapy

Advanced electrical stimulation techniques including TENS, ultrasound, and interferential therapy for pain relief and healing.

❄️ Cryotherapy

Cold therapy applications to reduce inflammation, manage pain, and accelerate recovery from acute injuries.

🔴 Laser Therapy

Low-level laser therapy to promote tissue healing, reduce inflammation, and provide pain relief at the cellular level.

💉 Dry Needling

Precise needle insertion into trigger points to release muscle tension and reduce pain in specific problem areas.

🎯 Cupping Therapy

Traditional cupping techniques combined with modern physiotherapy to improve blood flow and reduce muscle tension.
