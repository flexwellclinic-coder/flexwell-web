ABOUT FLEXWELL

DEDICATED TO YOUR RECOVERY

Our Story

Founded in 2015, FlexWell Physiotherapy has been at the forefront of rehabilitation and recovery in Dublin. Our clinic was born from a simple belief: everyone deserves to move pain-free and live their life to the fullest.

We combine evidence-based treatment methods with personalized care, ensuring each patient receives the attention and expertise they deserve. Our team of certified physiotherapists brings together decades of experience in sports medicine, rehabilitation, and holistic wellness.

From professional athletes to weekend warriors, from post-surgical recovery to chronic pain management, we've helped thousands of patients restore their mobility and reclaim their active lifestyles.

Frequently Asked Questions

What conditions do you treat?

We treat a wide range of conditions including sports injuries, back and neck pain, post-surgical rehabilitation, arthritis, stroke recovery, and chronic pain conditions.

Do I need a referral?

No referral is needed for physiotherapy treatment. However, some insurance plans may require a GP referral for coverage. We recommend checking with your insurance provider.

What should I expect in my first session?

Your first session includes a comprehensive assessment of your condition, medical history review, physical examination, and development of a personalized treatment plan.

How long are the sessions?

Initial consultations are 60 minutes, while follow-up sessions are typically 45 minutes. Treatment duration depends on your individual needs and progress.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we work with most major insurance providers including VHI, Laya Healthcare, and Irish Life Health. Please bring your insurance details to your appointment.

What are your cancellation policies?

We require at least 24 hours notice for cancellations. Late cancellations or no-shows may incur a fee as outlined in our terms and conditions.
